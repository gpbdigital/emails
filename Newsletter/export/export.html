Creative Update
 
Whatever the lockdown situation, creativity gets us through. We are very happy to share some of the recent work that gets us out of bed on these dark mornings.
Thank you to all our client partners who have been so supportive in such novel circumstances. We’re already working on many exciting projects that we can’t wait to bring you in 2021.

Virgin Media
Welcome to the Virgin Media Wellness Retreat... for TVs.
Our multi-channel campaign launches TV360, Virgin Media’s upgraded game-changing TV box, which will help people rejuvenate their underperforming TVs and live together in electric dreams.
This is the first major outing of a new brand idea and line we’ve developed for Virgin Media, ‘Bring on Amazing’.

HSE
Keep your guard up.
With COVID-19 cases rising by the day and the whole country moving towards Level 5 Restrictions, we were tasked with empowering the Irish public to do everything they can to limit the spread of the virus. So we made an invisible threat more tangible and reminded people what happens when we drop our guard.

Standard Life
The End of Career Guidance Counsellor
To help solve the very real problem of retirement anxiety we partnered with Standard Life to create a new role in the organisation, someone dedicated to guiding customers towards a successful Second Life. And who was chosen to fill the position? Naturally the country’s top guidance guru. Meet Standard Life’s newest recruit, The End of Career Guidance Counsellor.

Virgin Media
Speed Obsessed
With the introduction of 1Gig Broadband from Virgin Media, Ireland’s fastest broadband network became even faster. To launch the new product, we got Emmy Award winning actor Brian Cox to narrate epic audio stories of the speed obsessives who also wanted to become even faster. We then went on a mission to see if anyone could beat the world record for speed blinking with our Instagram AR Effect ‘Blink Fast’.

Flogas
No need to hibernate for these Three Bears
Staying warm when you live off the gas grid can be quite a challenge. Flogas has the solution with a fully integrated LPG energy system. And who better to tell us how well it works than the Three Bears who live deep in the woods. The first campaign for our new client (welcome aboard!) brings this fairy tale to life with long form radio and smart digital executions.

#ComeIn
Last year we worked with BeLong To Youth Services, IAPI and an interagency team to support LGBTI+ people and develop the #ComeIn campaign around the belief that nobody would have to come out if everyone chose to come in. This year during Stand Up Awareness Week, we gave people the chance to show their support and solidarity in a new way, by using the #ComeIn icon as their canvas.
